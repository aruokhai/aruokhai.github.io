Can a Routing Node Collude With A Bolt13 Compliant Selfish Third Party Watchtower To Defraud An End Node ?

There are primarily two types of nodes in the Lightning Network at this time: routing nodes and end nodes. End Nodes are in charge of initiating and receiving payments, whereas Routing Nodes — which may or may not be altruistic — are in charge of routing payments. There are two types of watchtowers: nonprofit and for-profit, the latter of which generates income through a subscription and bounty scheme based on the Bolt 13 specification. I propose an attack which involves a Routing Node colluding with a For Profit Third Party Watchtower service to suppress the publication of a justice transaction in the event that a specific commitment transaction is discovered on the chain.
